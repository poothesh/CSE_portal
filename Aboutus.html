About the Department

    The Department of Computer Science and Engineering was established in the year 2000 and it offers a four-year undergraduate program in Computer Science and Engineering. The Department is located in a newly constructed 70000 Sq.ft high-tech Computer Science and Information Technology block. The Computer Science and Information Technology block also includes a separate laboratory block and an Open Air Theatre with a capacity to accommodate 500 students. The Department has a separate library of more than 1000 books, international journals, national journals, magazines, back volumes, project reports and lecture CDs. The department maintains an exclusive question bank library comprising a vast collection of courses related to computer science and engineering. The department has a good record of student placements in national, international, and multinational companies.

Vision

To create computer professionals with a strong academic and technical background, achieving distinction at the national and international levels, while serving and leading society.

Mission

  • Provide excellent learning opportunities in Computer Science and Engineering.
  • Establish research centers addressing societal needs.
  • Develop ICT-based solutions for national development.

Programme Educational Objectives

  • PEO1: Graduates will comprehend mathematics, science, and engineering fundamentals, developing proficiency in computer-based engineering tools.
  • PEO2: Graduates will communicate and work effectively on multidisciplinary projects, adhering to professional ethics and social responsibility.
  • PEO3: Graduates will engage in lifelong learning, becoming experts as entrepreneurs or employees, expanding their domain knowledge.

Programme Outcomes (POs)

  • PO1: Apply mathematics, science, and engineering fundamentals to solve complex problems.
  • PO2: Identify, analyze, and solve complex engineering problems using research and literature review.
  • PO3: Design solutions for engineering problems considering public health, safety, societal, and environmental factors.
  • PO4: Conduct research and investigations to provide valid conclusions.
  • PO5: Use modern engineering and IT tools for complex engineering activities.
  • PO6: Assess societal, health, safety, legal, and cultural issues in professional engineering practice.
  • PO7: Understand the need for sustainable development in professional engineering solutions.
  • PO8: Commit to professional ethics and responsibilities.
  • PO9: Function effectively as an individual or team leader in diverse and multidisciplinary settings.
  • PO10: Communicate effectively in complex engineering activities.
  • PO11: Apply engineering and management principles to project management in multidisciplinary environments.
  • PO12: Engage in lifelong learning in the context of technological change.

Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs)

  • PSO1: Analyze, design, implement, test, and evaluate programs related to algorithms, networking, cloud computing, IoT, and data analytics.
  • PSO2: Develop innovative solutions for complex problems and identify research challenges in Computer Science and Engineering.
